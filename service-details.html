Neurology & Neurosurgery

Comprehensive care for brain, spine, and nervous system disorders. Advanced diagnostics, minimally invasive surgeries, and 24/7 stroke-ready team.

Overview

The Department of Neurology at Hospil is a state-of-the-art facility equipped with advanced neuroimaging, electrophysiology labs, and a dedicated neuro-ICU. Our multidisciplinary team provides compassionate, evidence-based care for all neurological conditions — from headaches to complex brain tumors.

Conditions We Treat

  • Spine Care Center

    Rapid response clot retrieval, thrombolysis, and rehabilitation.

  • Epilepsy

    Advanced EEG monitoring, medication & surgical options.

  • Spinal Disorders

    Herniated discs, spinal stenosis, minimally invasive spine surgery.

  • Parkinson's Disease

    Deep brain stimulation (DBS), movement disorder clinic.

  • Chronic Migraine

    Botulinum toxin, CGRP therapies & nerve blocks.

  • Brain Tumors

    Neuro-oncology, awake craniotomy, and targeted therapy.

Advanced Technology & Procedures

3T MRI & fMRI

High-resolution brain mapping for surgical planning.

Intraoperative Neuro Monitoring

Real-time nerve monitoring during spine/brain surgery.

Robotic Neurosurgery

Minimally invasive biopsies & DBS electrode placement.

neurovascular suite

Mechanical thrombectomy for acute stroke.
